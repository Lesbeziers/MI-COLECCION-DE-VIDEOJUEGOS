therealfafnir 2024-05-25 (8/10)
A great game, it's a classic cRPG with an AD&D like ruleset. It never gets old, like playing Pen&Paper, but the Commodore is your Dungeon Master. The riddles are strong tobacco, not only the magic mouthes but also cartographing the darkness zones (and all of the dungeons) give you a mistery, thrill and chill atmosphere. Play and daydream your personal story of the evil enchanted Skara Brae, captured in an eternal winter, seek out for the Mad God (What was it name? ;-)), fight the twofold Stone Golems (a Hint) and go wild with 4x99 Berserkers. Go forth and train your Mages until they have mastered every profession to become mighty archmages, helping your valor group to defeat evil Mangar. At least i vote 8 of 10 but back in time, it would be 9 or 10, for me.

Note: Press Cursor Right/Left Keys During Combat To Speed Up Scrolling. Keep the Key pressed until you see "Faster" (or "Slower" on Screen, so frying 396 creatures will go much much much faster. In the game manuals sometimes < > is printed, but it's the cursor keys! This is not available in all tape versions (some versions missing that function), with the disk version for sure... Have Fun and don't cheat multiplying the Fire Horn form El Cid ;-) or glitch in some extra money ;-)
